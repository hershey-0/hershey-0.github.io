This pie chart displays the major factors that impact the enhanced greenhouse effect in Australia. It is evident that energy sources are the main contributors to global warming, contributing to approximately 50% of the greenhouse effect.

These findings suggest that reducing emissions from energy production, transport, and agriculture could be key to mitigating Australia's greenhouse effect. Ultimately, the graph depicts the various sources that contribute to the greenhouse effect in Australia.
